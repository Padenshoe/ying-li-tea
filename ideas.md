### Idea 1: Wabi-Sabi Naturalism

**Design Movement:** Wabi-Sabi — finding beauty in imperfection, transience, and natural forms.

**Core Principles:**
- Organic asymmetry over rigid grids
- Texture and grain as visual language
- Negative space as a breathing room for the eye
- Handcrafted, imperfect edges and brush-stroke motifs

**Color Philosophy:** Warm cream (#F5F0E8), raw linen (#E8DFD0), muted sage (#8A9E7F), aged clay (#B07A5A), and deep charcoal (#2E2A26). Colors evoke dried tea leaves, ceramic glaze, and morning mist over mountains.

**Layout Paradigm:** Staggered, asymmetric sections. Hero has large left-aligned text with a floating image offset to the right. Product cards arranged in an irregular masonry-like pattern. Generous vertical rhythm.

**Signature Elements:**
- Subtle paper-grain texture overlay on sections
- Thin, hand-drawn-style divider lines
- Ink-wash watercolor illustrations as section accents

**Interaction Philosophy:** Slow, deliberate hover transitions. Elements "breathe" — subtle scale and opacity shifts. Scroll-triggered fade-ins that feel like mist clearing.

**Animation:** 0.6s ease-out fade + slight upward drift on scroll reveal. Hover on cards: gentle warm shadow bloom. No jarring transitions.

**Typography System:** `Cormorant Garamond` (display, light 300 for headings) paired with `Source Serif 4` (body, regular 400). Headings are large and airy; body text is compact and readable.

### Idea 2: Zen Modernism — Chosen Design

**Design Movement:** Japanese Minimalism meets Taiwanese tea culture — clean structure, purposeful emptiness, subtle cultural nods.

**Core Principles:**
- Maximum restraint — every element earns its place
- Horizontal rhythm and quiet vertical breathing
- Cultural texture through typography and micro-details
- Premium through absence, not addition

**Color Philosophy:** Warm white (#FAFAF7), cream (#F2EDE4), pale sage (#C8D5C0), soft moss (#6B7F5E), warm charcoal (#3A3530), and muted gold (#C4A96A) as a rare accent. This palette evokes the color of a porcelain cup, fresh oolong, and mountain fog.

**Layout Paradigm:** Wide, horizontal sections with generous padding. Hero is full-bleed with centered text floating over a misty background. About section uses a 60/40 text-image split. Products in a clean 3-column grid with lots of breathing room.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rules (1px, warm gray) as section dividers
- Subtle Chinese/Taiwanese character motifs as decorative accents
- Soft gradient washes from cream to white between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:** Understated elegance. Hover states are whisper-quiet — a thin underline, a soft color shift. Nothing shouts.

**Animation:** Gentle fade-up on scroll (0.5s, ease-out). Navbar becomes slightly frosted on scroll. Product cards lift with a 2px shadow on hover.

**Typography System:** `Playfair Display` (display, italic for taglines) paired with `Lato` (body, 300/400 weight). Brand name in a custom thin-weight serif. Letter-spacing on headings for an airy, premium feel.

### Idea 3: Ink & Stone Elegance

**Design Movement:** East Asian Ink Painting aesthetics — monochromatic depth, bold contrast, meditative stillness.

**Core Principles:**
- Stark contrast between ink-dark and paper-white
- Calligraphic energy in type and line
- Sparse composition with one focal point per section
- Silence as design — large empty areas are intentional

**Color Philosophy:** Near-white paper (#F8F5F0), deep ink (#1A1714), warm stone (#7A6E64), faded sepia (#C9B99A), and a single accent of muted jade (#5C7A6A). Inspired by traditional ink wash paintings and aged paper.

**Layout Paradigm:** Dramatic full-height sections. Hero features a single large calligraphic brand name on a near-white field. Sections alternate between ink-dark backgrounds and paper-white. Products displayed in a sparse 2-column layout.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large-scale decorative Chinese characters as background watermarks
- Ink-stroke SVG dividers between sections
- High-contrast product photography frames

**Interaction Philosophy:** Bold and deliberate. Hover states are high-contrast — elements snap to dark/light inversions. Cursor changes to a custom circle.

**Animation:** Dramatic reveal animations — text slides in from left like a brushstroke. Section transitions use a horizontal wipe effect. 0.4s ease-in-out.

**Typography System:** `Noto Serif SC` (display, for cultural authenticity) paired with `IBM Plex Serif` (body). Headings are large and commanding; body text is small and precise.

## Selected Design: **Idea 2 — Zen Modernism**

Chosen for its balance of cultural refinement and modern minimalism. The warm cream-to-sage palette, generous whitespace, and Playfair Display + Lato typography system will create a premium, calming experience that honors Taiwanese tea culture without being decorative or heavy.
